Overall Floor Area: 130 Sq. Metres (1,399 Sq. Feet)

Property Description:

Delightful period cottage style residence home of the renowned Irish folk artist Johnny McEvoy, situated in this much sought after location with large old world back garden and rere access. Gas fired central heating. 10'6" high ceilings in the livingroom. Double glazed windows. Ornamental coving, 64' rere garden. In need of T.L.C.

LOCATION

Close to the Dodder River and Milltown Park, Dundrum Town Centre all schools and churches. On a main bus route and approx. 3.2 km from the city centre.

A range of trendy bars, cafes and restaurants are on the doorstep. Milltown River Park is less than a two minute walk. An abundance of excellent local schools in the area including Muckross Park College, Gonzaga, Sandford Park and St.Mary's National School, Belmont Avenue. Dublin city centre is easily accessible with regular bus services along both Clonskeagh and Ranelagh Roads.

OUTSIDE

Old world large back garden. Paved sun terrace. Crazy paving pathway to rere access. Many mature trees, hedges, shrubs and flower borders. Garden shed.

SERVICES

Mains drainage, Water, Gas, Electricity, Alarm.


DIRECTIONS
